Kevin's Journey with Coco

Hello everyone! This is Kevin, and my dog Coco. He is such a sweet puppy. I want to share my journey with Coco here with everyone. Last month Coco got sick, and I have to take care of him. Here are some advice when your dog is sick. You have to help groom your dogs because they would not have the energy to take care of themselves. Always keep them plenty of fresh water. They may not have apetite to eat the regular dog food, so you can cook some soupy rice for them because it is easy to digest. Also, when necessary, please take your dogs to the vet and listen to vetenarians' advice!
